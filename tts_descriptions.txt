# Feedback Modality stuff
Feedback Modality: The feedback modality you will be using is
Audio Beacon: Audio Beacon
Responsive Audio Beacon: Responsive Audio Beacon
Responsive Audio Beacon + Haptics: Responsive Audio Beacon + Haptics

Audio Beacon Description: Depending where the beacon is located, you will hear a hum either on the left or right side of your ear. If you feel it in both ears evenly, it means you are roughly facing the object head on.
Responsive Audio Beacon Description: There are four tones whose volume and pitch change based on direction. When you face a beacon directly, both are strongest. Be sure to check up and down as well as left and right for the best signal.
Haptics Description: When you are within two meters of the beacon, your controllers start vibrating. The vibration changes with distance and especially with how accurately you are aiming, so rely on it more than the hum to pinpoint the beacons position.
